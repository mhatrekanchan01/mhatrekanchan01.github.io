About

HR that is warm with people and precise with process.

My Story

A career built across HR foundations, U.S. operations, and global people support.

My HR foundation began in recruitment, HR operations, and business partnering, where I learned how much trust depends on follow-through, documentation, and clear communication. Over time, my work expanded into employee lifecycle processes, manager support, benefits, leave, compliance, dashboards, and workflow improvement.

Today, I support distributed teams across NAM, LATAM, and Europe. I enjoy work that asks HR to be both human and operational: listening carefully, organizing ambiguity, clarifying ownership, and building systems that make good HR easier to deliver consistently.

I am especially drawn to companies that value AI-forward thinking in HR: not as a shortcut around judgment, but as a way to reduce repetitive work, improve documentation, and give people teams better visibility.
